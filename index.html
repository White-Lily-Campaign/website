About

The White Lily Campaign is a non-partisan, volunteer based campaign that strives to mobilize Canadians, from coast to coast, who together will lend their voices in saying no to Child Poverty.

How in 2019, when Canada enjoys records level of national wealth are 1 in 6 children forced to go to bed hungry?
